Selecting an Exam

Choose the correct exam:

  1. Select the Pick Your Organization drop-down list and select the name of your organization.
  2. Select the Pick Your Teacher drop-down list and select the name of your teacher.
  3. Select the Pick Your Exam drop-down list and select the name of the exam you wish to take.

You will need to fill out your personal information:

Enter your first name, last name, email address and phone number in the fields provided. Please ensure that the email address if valid.

Select the Next button to continue to the authentication process.

The phone number is an optional field and is for support purposes only. If one isn't provided, we will try to reach you by email should support be necessary.
